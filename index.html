About

Dope Gallery was created in 2013 to connect collectors with limited edition prints, originals, sculptures and rare objects from some of the most collected contemporary and urban artists. Every work offered is vetted for authenticity and condition, with a focus on blue-chip names and carefully selected emerging voices.

We work with private collectors, galleries and dealers worldwide, placing works into homes, offices and collections across the globe. All shipments are packed with care and fully insured.

Sell / Consign
Submit works for sale or consignment.

We are always interested in acquiring or consigning works by key contemporary and urban artists — from single pieces to whole collections. Send us details of what you have available and we’ll come back with options for outright purchase or consignment.

  • Include artist, title, year and medium
  • Add edition number and size for prints
  • Upload clear photos of front, back and signature
  • Mention original invoice or provenance where available
Payment & Delivery

Payments

  • Bank transfer and major credit cards accepted
  • Crypto payments available on request (BTC / ETH and selected stablecoins)
  • Invoices issued in EUR / USD / GBP depending on location

Shipping

  • Worldwide shipping with tracking and insurance
  • Professional packing for framed and unframed works
  • Typical dispatch time: 3–7 working days after payment
  • Customs and import duties vary by country and are buyer’s responsibility
